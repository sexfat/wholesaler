The Order Center is the control center for EZ order.

Selling
step1Setting
Preparation before Selling:

Before launching your online shop, you need to fill in some basic blanks, including your company profile, payment account, contact person, your logistics company, flat rate shipping, item-based or weight-based shipping method, returns & refunds policy, and certain countries excluded for delivery.

step2 Products
add products:

This function enables you to search, check, group and update product information. Before listing a product online, please prepare for your product high-quality picture, video, product feature, detailed product description and competitive price.

step3 Orders
Manage Orders:

This is for you not only to check all the order details including ordered items and amount, but also to update order status--undelivered, pending, delivered, cancelled, refunded, and so on.

step4 account
account Management:

This works as a monthly report for you to check order amount, refund record, commission, and account balance. Those who have Paypal account can directly withdraw money deducing service charge

step5 after-Sales Service
Qa Center:

You are advised to customize your own FaQ content and title to lower your customer service cost”

Feedback Center:

To understand the comments from clients on the website function and hence increase web service quality

Dispute Center:

To manage deal disputes and improve clients' satisfaction.
