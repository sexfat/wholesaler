Shipping Area

Shipping Area allows you to specify shipping rates based on location. For example, if you are selling from the US, you might create a Standard Shipping Area that contains a rate for shipping within the US, and an Everywhere Else for shipping outside of the US.

You are advised to calculate your shipping fee based on your product’s weight by kilogram. Also, our shipping fee calculator will calculate by default the best shipping program when our buyers pick up more than one item and check out.

If your products are bigger based on parcel size, you are advised to set your product’s weight by volume weight
